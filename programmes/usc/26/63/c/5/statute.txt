26 U.S. Code § 63(c)(5) — Limitation on basic standard deduction in the case of certain dependents

In the case of an individual with respect to whom a deduction under section
151 is allowable to another taxpayer for a taxable year beginning in the
calendar year in which the individual's taxable year begins, the basic
standard deduction applicable to such individual for such individual's
taxable year shall not exceed the greater of —
  (A) $500, or
  (B) the sum of $250 and such individual's earned income.

Inflation adjustments per § 63(c)(4) produce the following 2026 amounts:
  * Clause (A) minimum: $1,350
  * Clause (B) additional earned-income component: $450
  * Basic standard deduction cap (single filer, § 63(c)(2)): $16,100
