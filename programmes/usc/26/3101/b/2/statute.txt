26 U.S. Code § 3101(b)(2) — Additional Medicare Tax

(b) Hospital insurance
  (2) Additional tax
    In addition to the tax imposed by paragraph (1) and the preceding sentence
    of this paragraph, there is hereby imposed on the income of every
    individual a tax equal to 0.9 percent of wages (as defined in section
    3121(a)) which are received with respect to employment (as defined in
    section 3121(b)) —
      (A) in the case of a joint return, to the extent such wages exceed
          $250,000,
      (B) in the case of a married taxpayer (as defined in section 7703)
          filing a separate return, to the extent such wages exceed $125,000,
          and
      (C) in any other case, to the extent such wages exceed $200,000.
